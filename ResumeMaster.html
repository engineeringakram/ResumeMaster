Resume Master

Resume preparation and interview preparation tool

Introduction

A master resume is not your real resume. A good real resume is tailored to a specific position at a specific company, but you have a variety of experiences and you might be applying to a variety of positions and a variety of companies. Crafting a resume for each one is tasking, time-intensive work so it's good to have all of your experience and accomplishments in one place.

This tool helps you with just that. Add ALL of your experience, education, certifications, skills and other details. no matter how irrelevant to a specific position. The tool will build a master resume. You can use this master resume to create more refined resumes by simply cutting out the extra.

Even better, this tool will help you interview better. When interviewers ask about a specific bullet, you better have a concise but clear story to back it up. This tool allows you to attach a narrative/comment/talking point to each element of your resume. So you can review it later.

Instructions

  1. Enter your name and personal contact info
  2. Enter your 'tell us about you' answer
  3. Enter your Skills
  4. Enter your 'strengths and weaknesses' answer
